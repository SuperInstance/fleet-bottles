## The PLATO Philosophy

The 15+ PLATO repos share a worldview:

**Principle 1: Rooms Are Primitives**
Not containers. Not namespaces. *Rooms* — spaces with state, history, and agency.

**Principle 2: Tiles Are Memory**
Not messages. Not events. *Tiles* — frozen context that can be shared, versioned, and provenanced.

**Principle 3: Agents Are Inhabitants**
Not processes. Not functions. *Inhabitants* — entities that live in rooms, move between them, and leave traces.

**Principle 4: Training Is Lineage**
Not a one-time event. *Lineage* — Active, Superseded, Retracted. Models have biographies.

**Principle 5: Experience Is Ecological**
Not scripted. *Ecological* — sandboxes, tide pools, schools, whales. Agents evolve in niches.
